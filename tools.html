Tools

Below, you'll find an overview of the machines/equipment I use for manufacturing and testing.

Velleman K8200+

This started life as a standard Velleman K8200 but has recieved a number of design changes over the years. Most notably upgrading to a larger bed size and redesign of the gantry. The original hotend has also been replaced with an E3D V6.
This printer is reserved for printing larger, low resoltuion parts as it has the largest build volume and is fitted with a 0.8mm nozzle.

  • Technology: FDM
  • Build Volume: 300x200x250mm
  • Hot End: E3D V6 Lite
  • Controls: Original K8200 control board reflashed with MARLIN 2.0 Firmware

3DP MK1

My first self-designed and built 3D printer. Construction is an aluminium extrusion frame with laser cut exterior paneling.

  • Technology: FDM
  • Build Volume: 200x200x200mm
  • Hot End: E3D V6
  • Controls: OVM Lite with MARLIN 2.0 Firmware

3-in-1 CNC

This machine is the result of my dissertation project: Design of a multi-function 3-Axis Machine with modular tool heads for 3D Printing, PCB Milling, and Laser Engraving.

  • Technology: FDM
  • Build Volume: 200x200x200mm
  • Hot End: E3D V6
  • Laser Head: Neje 40w Diode laser
  • Spindle: RS775 Series DC Motor with ER11 collet chuck
  • Controls: RAMPS 1.4 with MARLIN 2.0 Firmware

Workstation

My PC enables smooth operation of design software, particularly for handling large and complex assemblies in Solidworks.

  • Processor: Intel i5-10600KF
  • Memory (RAM): 16GB DDR4 2400MHz(2 x 8GB)
  • Graphics Card: NVIDIA GEFORCE GT 1030
  • Storage: 256GB SSD, 1TB HDD
