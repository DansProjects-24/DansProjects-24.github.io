Welcome to my digital workshop!

I'm a mechanical design engineer, 3D printing enthusiast, general maker of things and a University of Brighton graduate.
Beyond the confines of my professional responsibilities, my free time is spent working on a variety of projects which primarily involve CAD, 3D Printing, and Arduino's.
On this website, you'll find a collection of my projects, showcasing the design and development process and accompanied by any design files for those interested in making it themselves.
This platform serves as both a portfolio of my achievements and a way to share my projects with makers and enthusiasts alike.

3D Design

As a Certified Solidworks Professional I use my extensive knowledge of the software to create parts and assemblies for my projects.

Manufacturing

With the use of 3D Printing, CNC machining, or Laser engraving I can quickly transform a digital design into a prototype.

Electronic Design

Drawing upon my electronics knowledge, I can create electrical schematics and PCB layouts to bring automation to my projects.

PCB Assembly

I assemble and test my PCBs in house with attention to detail ensuring even the smallest SMD components are assembled correctly.

Latest Projects

I began documenting my projects on Instagram in 2017 and continue to post updates when I find time to work on them. Follow my Instagram account to stay updated on my latest projects and see behind-the-scenes content.
