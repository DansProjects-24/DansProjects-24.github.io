Welcome to my digital workshop!

I am a mechanical design engineer, 3D printing enthusiast, and general maker of things. I graduated from the University of Brighton and now work as a mechanical design engineer.
Beyond the confines of my professional responsibilities, my free time is spent working on a variety of projects which primarily involve CAD, 3D Printing, and Arduino Programming.
On this website, you'll find a collection of my projects, showcasing the design and development process and accompanied by any design files for those interested in making it themselves.
This platform serves as both a portfolio of my achievements and a way to share my projects with makers and enthusiasts alike.
